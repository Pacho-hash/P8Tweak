## ✨ Features

- **⚙️ System Tweaks**: Safely disable unnecessary background services, animations, and bloated Windows features to improve responsiveness.
- **🛡️ Privacy**: Reclaim your data by turning off telemetry, personalized ads, and background data collection with a single click.
- **🎮 Gaming**: Optimize network priority, disable CPU-hogging background tasks, and optimize thread priority for ultra-smooth gaming sessions.
- **🌐 Network**: Tune TCP/IP settings, DNS cache, and network adapter configurations to lower latency and increase download speeds.
- **🧹 Cleanup**: Thoroughly clean temporary files, cache, and leftover junk that basic disk cleanup tools miss.
- **🏥 Drive Health**: Monitor your SSD/HDD's S.M.A.R.T. status, temperature, and wear-level in real time.
- **🗂️ Storage Analyzer**: Visually identify what's eating up your disk space and easily delete large, forgotten files.
- **⏱️ Benchmark**: Test your CPU, RAM, and Disk performance directly within the app to measure your optimization gains.
- **🚀 Max Optimize**: Our signature one-click solution to apply the best, safest performance tweaks across your entire system simultaneously.
-  Over 40+ system tweaks and optimizations!

## 💻 System Requirements

- **OS:** Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit only)
- **RAM:** 4 GB minimum (8 GB recommended)
- **Storage:** 150 MB available space
- **Framework:** .NET 10.0 or higher

## 📥 How to Install

1. Download the latest installer from our [Releases Page](https://github.com/Pacho-hash/P8Tweak/releases/latest).
2. Run the `P8Tweak-Installer.exe` file.
3. Follow the standard Inno Setup installation wizard.
4. Launch P8 Tweak from your Start Menu or Desktop shortcut!

## 🔑 How to Activate

P8 Tweak requires a license key for full access. 

1. Upon launching the application, you will be greeted by the activation screen.
2. Enter your purchased serial key in the designated field.
3. Click **Activate**. The app will securely verify your license, and you're good to go!
